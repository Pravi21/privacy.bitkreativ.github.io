Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Textlytics AI (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is an Android application designed to help users monitor and understand their financial activity by securely analyzing transaction-related SMS messages received on their device.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Textlytics AI collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Children’s Privacy (Under 13)

Textlytics AI is safe for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children.

The app processes SMS messages locally on the device to identify financial transactions. We do not build personal profiles of children, display targeted ads, or sell user data.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has shared personal information with us unintentionally, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

2. Information We Collect

SMS Data

Textlytics AI reads SMS messages only to detect financial transactions such as bank alerts, UPI confirmations, or wallet notifications.

  • Only transaction-related content is processed
  • Personal messages are ignored
  • Messages are not stored permanently on our servers

Device & App Data

We may collect limited non-personal information such as:

  • App version
  • Android OS version
  • Anonymous error logs (for app stability)

3. Permissions We Use & Why

Textlytics AI requires the following Android permissions:

Essential Permissions

  • READ_SMS / RECEIVE_SMS – To detect and analyze transaction messages
  • SEND_SMS – Used only if required by underlying telephony components (not for messaging users)
  • INTERNET – To sync insights securely with cloud services

Background Processing

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / DATA_SYNC – To process SMS reliably
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – To restart monitoring after device reboot
  • WAKE_LOCK – To ensure message processing completes correctly
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS – To prevent missed transactions
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM – For precise task scheduling

Notifications & UI

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – To notify users of detected transactions
  • BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE – To read transaction alerts from apps that don’t send SMS
  • VIBRATE – For notification feedback

4. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Detect and categorize financial transactions
  • Provide spending insights
  • Improve app reliability and accuracy

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Limited anonymized data may be processed using secure cloud services (such as Firebase or Cloudflare Workers) strictly for app functionality and analytics.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted communication
  • Minimal data retention
  • Secure cloud infrastructure

7. User Control

You can:

  • Disable SMS permissions at any time via Android settings
  • Uninstall the app to stop all data processing
